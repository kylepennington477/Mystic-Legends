Mystic Legends - Code of Honor

Community First. Fun Always.

Our number one rule is simple: Have fun. We’re building a community grounded in respect, fair play, and camaraderie. Whether you're here to climb the ranks or just enjoy a great game -- you're part of something legendary.

Player Identity Guidelines

Any player name that may be considered inappropriate or offensive will be temporarily shielded. If your ID is boxed, please contact an Admin for assistance in updating your name.

Respectful Conduct

Profanity, fighting, and accusations are not tolerated. Disagreements should be handled calmly through proper channels. Honor your fellow legends.

Behavior Enforcement

Players who use profanity, engage in threatening behavior, or create a hostile environment will be shielded and removed if the behavior continues.

Match Commitment

Leaving games or intentionally disqualifying repeatedly will result in penalties. Legends see battles through.

Zero Tolerance for Cheating

Cheating of any kind will result in immediate disciplinary action. Victory must be earned with honor.

Resign Button Policy

Do not use the Resign button unless absolutely necessary. Legends don’t back down from a challenge.

One Account Per Player

Players are allowed one account only. Multiple accounts will lead to removal or restriction.

Slow Play Policy

Each round is capped at 45 minutes. Deliberate delays are not tolerated.

Table Creation Responsibility

The first player listed must create the table. If not done within 2 minutes, the second player is expected to set it up.

MIA Timer

If a player is inactive for more than 5 minutes, they may forfeit the match. Timeliness matters.

Multi-Game Limit

Players may enter up to 3 games at a time. If this results in slow play, you will be asked to DQ and limited to one game moving forward.

No Spectating or Kibitzing

Watching games you are not actively playing in is not permitted. Keep the arena fair and focused.

SHG Messenger Requirement

Players must appear Online on SHG Messenger while in the lobby. Those on DND will not be allowed to participate in games or tournaments.

ID Consistency

Your Safe Harbor Games ID and Mystic Legends ID must be identical. Inconsistencies may restrict your participation.

Reporting Matches

Both players must report the win and the loss in the lobby after each game. Transparency is key.
